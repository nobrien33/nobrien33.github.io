about

Nick O’Brien is a GIS Specialist and Cartographer who partners with civil engineers, environmental scientists and project managers to assist them with desktop analysis, spatial data management, creating thematic maps. After spending nearly a decade working in the restaurant industry, Nick decided to return to school to study his passion: geography and maps. He believes that all maps tell a story, it is up to the viewer to decide what that is.

Nick holds a BS in geography from the University of Wisconsin – River Falls and is currently working on a MS in GIS and web map programming at the University of Wisconsin - Madison. Nick lives in Hudson, Wisconsin with his wife and two kids.
